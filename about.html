Hi! I'm Jennifer (or Duyen), a designer and illustrator based in Melbourne.

My mission is to make the world a little bit more colourful and a little bit more accessible. Through my background in UI/UX and graphic design and my passion for illustration, I translate creative design concepts into memorable end products that anyone can use. In my free time, I like to work on original worldbuilding projects and go birding.

In the past, I've worked on Farrago and other independent publications.
