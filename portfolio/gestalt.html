Gestalt

Gestalt is a modular typeface created from digitised ink prints made using shapes cut from sponges, giving it a bold and playful feel. It's a perfect typeface for when you need to be loud and express yourself.

Timeframe: 3 weeks

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign
