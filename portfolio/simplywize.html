Simply Wize

I worked with Momentum Foods in order to improve the digital shelf presence of Simply Wize, a gluten-free food brand specializing in frozen meals and pantry snacks. These graphics are intended for major retail platforms (Coles, Woolworths, and Amazon) and have been designed according to their product guidelines.

Timeframe: 6 weeks

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Canva

Skills: Graphic Design, Branding, Marketing
